About Balaji Action Buildwell Private Limited

Balaji Action Buildwell Private Limited, operating under the brand name Action TESA, is a prominent manufacturer in India's panel industry. Established in 2009, the company has its state-of-the-art manufacturing facility in Sitarganj, Uttarakhand. Known for its wide array of engineered wood products like Plain Particle Boards, MDF Boards, and Decorative Wall Panels, Action TESA has marked several industry-first milestones. It was the first in India to install a thin MDF/HDF plant, introduce DOME Technology, and manufacture True HDF-Based Laminate Wooden Flooring. Additionally, it pioneered the launch of UV High Gloss Panels in the Indian market. The Sitarganj plant is equipped with advanced German technology, notably the 7th generation Contiroll from Seimpelkamp GmbH & Co., making Action TESA the largest MDF manufacturer in India, with an annual capacity of 400,000 cubic meters. The company's product portfolio includes Pre-laminated Particle Boards, High Gloss Acrylic Boards, UV Coated Boards, HDHMR Boards, and more—serving diverse needs in furniture and interior design. Action TESA is deeply committed to sustainability. It runs large-scale plantation drives, distributes high-yield eucalyptus saplings to farmers at subsidized rates, and offers technical guidance, promoting environmental conservation and farmer welfare. This helps increase green cover, enhances carbon sequestration, and ensures a sustainable raw material supply chain. As of 2024, the company employs around 2,240 individuals and has been recognized as a Great Place to Work, emphasizing its dedication to employee satisfaction and a positive work culture. In conclusion, Balaji Action Buildwell Private Limited, through Action TESA, continues to lead the Indian panel industry with innovation, sustainability, and world-class manufacturing excellence.
